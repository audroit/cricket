Overview

Cricket Trump Card is a mobile game. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data the app and its third-party services may process and why.

Data We Process

  • Analytics & diagnostics (Firebase Analytics). Aggregated usage events (e.g., screens viewed, session counts), crash logs, and basic device data needed to improve stability and features. No precise location is collected.
  • Cloud features (Firebase Realtime Database / Authentication). Anonymous IDs may be used for multiplayer rooms and game sync. The app currently uses anonymous authentication; we do not ask you to enter personal information.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob). AdMob may collect device identifiers and show personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your settings and regional requirements.

How We Use Data

  • To operate the game (e.g., multiplayer synchronization, basic telemetry).
  • To analyze app performance and fix crashes.
  • To display ads (if enabled in your region and settings).

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Realtime Database, Authentication)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These providers process data under their own privacy terms. See: Google Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for users aged 13+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Diagnostic and analytics data are retained by our providers for periods necessary to provide their services and maintain app quality.

Your Choices

Security
